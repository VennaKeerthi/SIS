A Student Information System (SIS) is a comprehensive software application that is designed to manage and organize information related to students in educational institutions. It serves as a centralized platform for storing, retrieving, and managing student data throughout their academic lifecycle. The primary purpose of a Student Information System is to streamline administrative tasks, enhance communication, and improve overall efficiency in educational institutions.

Here are the details included in this Student Information System project:

Registration Number,
Name,
Email ID,
Department,
Currently studying Semester,
Attendance Percentage,
CGPA


And the operations done in this Student Information System project are:

CRUD Operations :
Storing,
Retrieving,
Updating,
Deleting,
Displaying
